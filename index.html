About

MayMay is an independent artist blending atmospheric R&B, dream-pop textures, and vulnerable lyricism. Every release is crafted to feel cinematic, intimate, and lived-in.

From studio sessions to late-night writing rooms, this project is built on authenticity and emotional storytelling. The mission: connect deeply, move freely, and create music that lingers.
